# Product Brief: AI Escape

**Project Name:** AI Escape

## Executive Summary

AI Escape is an innovative, AI-driven puzzle game designed to address the limited replayability of traditional escape rooms. By leveraging AI to dynamically generate unique stories, puzzles, and environments, the game offers an endlessly replayable and highly immersive experience. The primary target audience is the 'tech-savvy puzzle enthusiast' who values intellectual challenge and novel gameplay. The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) will focus on delivering the core AI-driven narrative and puzzle generation, with a simple interaction model and visual system, to be completed within a 3-week timeline.

## 1. Problem Statement

Traditional puzzle games and escape rooms suffer from limited replayability and a static nature. Once solved, the experience is over. AI Escape aims to solve this by offering a dynamic, endlessly replayable, and highly immersive puzzle experience, where the AI generates unique stories, puzzles, and environments for each playthrough.

## 2. Proposed Solution

"AI Escape" is a highly immersive and customizable escape room game powered by AI. Players can tailor their experience by choosing themes, difficulty levels, and other parameters. The AI then generates a unique, tailor-made adventure, including the story, puzzles, and environment, ensuring that no two playthroughs are the same. The experience is enhanced by atmospheric visuals and sound effects that match the generated theme, creating a deeply immersive and personal puzzle-solving experience.

### Key Differentiators

*   **Deep Customization**: Players have a high degree of control over the type of experience they want.
*   **Dynamic AI Generation**: The core of the game is an AI that generates unique content for each playthrough, offering infinite replayability.
*   **High Immersiveness**: The combination of a unique story, atmospheric visuals, and sound effects creates a deeply immersive experience.



The primary user segment for AI Escape is represented by 'Chloe, the Curious Problem-Solver.' Chloe is a tech-savvy puzzle enthusiast, typically 25-44 years old, who is motivated by mental stimulation, innovation, and the satisfaction of solving complex problems. She values deep customization, infinite replayability, and immersive experiences, and is an early adopter of new technologies in gaming.

## 4. Success Metrics

Success for AI Escape will be measured by player engagement and satisfaction, specifically if players find the game fun and challenging. This indicates that the AI is effectively generating engaging puzzles and narratives, and that the core gameplay loop is compelling.

## 5. Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Scope

### Core MVP Features (Must-Haves for a working demo):

*   AI-Generated Story/Narrative
*   A Small Set of Puzzles (2-3 types)
*   Core Interaction Model (three options + go back)
*   Basic Visuals (pre-selected image library)
*   Basic Start Menu (Start New Game)

### Future Vision Features (Nice-to-Haves):

*   Hint Button
*   Background Music & Mute Option
*   Load/Save Game
*   Large Variety of Puzzles & Pictures
